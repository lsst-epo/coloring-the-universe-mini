  • Select M33 from the dropdown menu.
  • Turn on the u filter. You should see a greyscale image of M33.
  • Now click on the right side of the slider and select the color violet. You should now see the image turn violet.
  • Repeat for the remaining filters, assigning the colors in chromatic order.
Note: If you have difficulty distinguishing between certain colors, you can substitute alternate colors; magenta and cyan are available for this purpose.

You will now see a full color image of M33, made by combining the light from these six filters. For this image, the sliders are automatically positioned so that the colors are properly balanced (in other words, you donâ€™t see too much of one color.)
